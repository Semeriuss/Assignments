© Semere Habtu 2020

Semere loves collecting images and artworks that make the viewer
feel as if they are stepping into a dream. He is a student and part-time
art enthusiast residing in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He began
his journey through art at the age of 13. He then
went on to study for 4 years at Lideta Catholic Cathedral
School, graduating with a Secondary School Diploma in
2018. He is now pursuing a Software Engineering degree at
the Addis Ababa institute of Technology, Addis Ababa University

Semere hopes to help create a collection of breathtaking images
and artworks for all mediums be, commercial, domestic, creative + fashion.

For rates, bookings and all other enquires send Semere an email
or simply fill out the form provided below.

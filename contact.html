Semere loves collecting beautiful pictures and works of art that make the viewer feel as if they are stepping into a dream world. He is a student and part-time art enthusiast residing in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He began his long lasting pursuit of art at the age of 13 when he was in elementary school. He then went on to study for 4 years at the Lideta Catholic Cathedral School, graduating with a Secondary School Diploma in 2018.

Semere is currently seeking a Software Engineering degree at the Addis Ababa institute of Technology, the technological branch of Addis Ababa University. Semere hopes to help create a gallery of breathtaking works of artistic beauty through all mediums for public as well as private entertainment.

For questions, requests and all other enquires send Semere an email or simply fill out the form provided below.
